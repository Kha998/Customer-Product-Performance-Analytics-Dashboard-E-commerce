# Customer & Product Performance Analytics Dashboard (E-commerce)

An end-to-end **Business Intelligence project** built with **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze customer behavior, product profitability, and sales trends using the **Global Superstore dataset**.  
The project transforms raw transactional data into **actionable business insights** that support pricing, customer targeting, and product strategy decisions.


## Project Overview
This project focuses on evaluating **customer performance** and **product profitability** in an e-commerce context. Interactive dashboards were developed to help stakeholders understand what drives revenue, where profitability is created or lost, and how performance evolves over time. The analysis emphasizes business storytelling, KPI-driven insights, and time-based trend analysis to support data-driven decision-making.


## Objectives
- Analyze customer performance across segments, regions, and time  
- Evaluate product performance and profitability by category and sub-category  
- Identify high-value customers and top-performing products  
- Detect loss-making products and margin pressure  
- Monitor sales and profit trends using time-series and YoY growth analysis  

## Dashboards Included

### 1. Customer Segmentation & Performance Analytics
- Total Customers, Orders, Sales, Profit, and Profit Margin KPIs  
- Sales and profit contribution by customer segment  
- Monthly sales and profit trend analysis  
- Regional and geographic customer distribution  
- Top customers by revenue  

### 2. Product Performance, Profitability & Trend Analysis
- Total Products, Sales, Profit, and Profit Margin KPIs  
- Category and sub-category performance analysis  
- Product sales trend over time  
- Segment contribution to product demand  
- Top-selling and loss-making products

## Key Insights
- Sales show a consistent upward trend over time, while profit growth remains more moderate, indicating margin pressure.  
- Revenue is highly concentrated among a small group of customers and products, presenting both opportunity and risk.  
- Consumer customers drive the highest sales volume, while Corporate and Home Office segments demonstrate stronger profit efficiency.  
- Technology products lead in revenue contribution but show signs of margin pressure.  
- Several products consistently generate losses, highlighting opportunities for pricing optimization and product rationalization.  

## Business Recommendations
- Review pricing and discount strategies to improve profitability without slowing sales growth.  
- Prioritize retention and upselling efforts for high-value customer segments.  
- Reprice, bundle, or discontinue consistently loss-making products.  
- Invest in high-performing categories while improving cost efficiency in lower-margin areas.  
- Use time-based trends to improve demand forecasting, inventory planning, and marketing timing.
